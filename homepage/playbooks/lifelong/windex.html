Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup warm water

Instructions:
1. Mix all dry ingredients thoroughly.
2. Add oil and water, mix until dough forms.
3. Knead dough on a floured surface.
4. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
5. Shape, add toppings, and bake.
