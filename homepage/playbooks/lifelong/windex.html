        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        BEGINNER'S GUIDE TO TAI CHI: BASIC MOVEMENTS AND SEQUENCES
        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        This guide will take you through the foundational movements of Tai Chi, helping 
        to improve balance, flexibility, and mindfulness.

        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        MATERIALS NEEDED
        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        - Yoga mat
        - Comfortable clothing
        - Water bottle

        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS
        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        1. Warm-Up (5 minutes): Perform gentle stretches to prepare your body for exercise.
        2. Basic Stance (3 minutes): Practice the foundational Tai Chi stance.
        3. Movement Sequence (15 minutes): Follow these steps to perform the basic Tai Chi routine.

        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        TROUBLESHOOTING
        ------------------------------------------------------------------------------
        If you find it difficult to maintain balance in the Basic Stance, try adjusting 
        your foot positioning slightly wider.
