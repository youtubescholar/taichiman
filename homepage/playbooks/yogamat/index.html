About This Project

The Yoga Mat Series creatively combines martial arts techniques with the convenience of a yoga mat, specially designed for those with limited space.

Whether you're a martial arts novice or looking to maintain your practice in a smaller setting, the Yoga Mat Series offers a practical solution.

Explore how martial arts can be adapted to fit your space and lifestyle, no matter the size of your practice area.
