Assertiveness Training Module Guide
------------------------------------

1. Understanding Assertiveness:
   - What is assertiveness?
   - Difference between assertive, passive, and aggressive behavior.

2. Benefits of Being Assertive:
   - Helps in respecting your own rights and the rights of others.
   - Improves communication and reduces conflicts.

3. Key Assertive Techniques:
   - Use "I" statements to express your feelings without blaming others.
   - Practice saying "no" respectfully when necessary.
