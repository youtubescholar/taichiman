How to Get Here

Address: 123 Martial Arts St, City, State, ZIP

Directions:

From the city center, take Route 10 northbound and exit at Main St. The dojo is located two blocks east of the station. Look for the red brick building with the large "Martial Arts" sign.

Public Transport Options:

  • Bus: Take bus line 5 to Main St.
  • Subway: Blue Line to Martial Arts Station.

Landmarks:

Near the Central Park entrance and across from the Main Library.

Parking:

Free parking is available in the lot adjacent to the building. Overflow parking is available on 2nd St.

Accessibility:

The facility is wheelchair accessible with ramps and elevators. Please contact us if you require any assistance.

Online Class Instructions

Join our online classes via Zoom. Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a safe space for training.

Steps to Join:

  1. Register for the class through our platform.
  2. Receive a Zoom link via email.
  3. Join the class 5 minutes before the start time.

Online Safety Tips:

  • Ensure your training area is free from hazards.
  • Wear appropriate attire and have necessary equipment ready.
  • Mute your microphone unless speaking to avoid distractions.
