Welcome!

Let's pause for a while and gather our thoughts. Here on this site I'm providing my own perspective regarding Tai Chi Chuan, martial arts, exercise, health promotion, and wellness. It's important to have your own perspective so take me with a grain of salt. Let's apperciate that the world is a pretty big place and that rome wasn't built in a day. There is a lot of material, there is a lot to do, often we don't have enough time to do what we want or even need. All of that is intimidating and can prevent action being taken. So enjoy this cute slideshow! It's my reminder and my promise to lower the intimidation factor as much as possible when we're doing what's meaningful.
